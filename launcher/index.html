Touhou Launcher

The improved version of the old and outdated Touhou Launcher made by Widdiful.

Features

  • Works with PC98 games too! (emulator Neko Project II included, but do not tell anyone!)
  • You can open a game's custom.exe (or config.exe for Touhou 7.5) within the launcher.
  • Wrap up all your Touhou games in the same place so you do not have tons of shortcuts!
  • You can also do it with fan games because we know some of you have tons; so do not worry, we got you covered.
  • This also works with the Touhou Community Reliant Automatic Patcher (the launcher will search for the custom.exe of the english patch).
  • Comes with an auto update feature so you do not have to worry about having to come here dozens of times ;)
  • Download replays for your favourite games just by copying its url!
  • Music player to listen to the bestest of the best!
  • Read some manga whenever you want!

F.A.Q

The answer is yes, the Launcher supports non-static patches (e.g. thcrap); in fact, we would recommend not to use static patches.
