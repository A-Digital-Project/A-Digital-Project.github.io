Touhou Launcher

The improved version of the old and outdated Touhou Launcher made by Widdiful.

Features

  • Works with PC98 games too! (emulator (Neko Project II) and games included, but do not tell anyone!)
  • You can open a game's custom.exe (or config.exe for Touhou 7.5, may be ZUN thought it was funny to change the name, but it isn't -_-) within the launcher.
  • Wrap up all your Touhou games in the same place so you do not have tons of shortcuts!
  • You can also do it with fan games because we know some of you have tons, too; so do not worry, we got you covered.
  • This also works with the Touhou Community Reliant Automatic Patcher (the launcher will search for the custom.exe of the english patch).
  • Comes with an auto update feature so you do not have to worry about having to come here dozens of times ;)
  • Download replays for your favourite games, you can do it just by copying a replay's url!

F.A.Q

The answer is yes, the Launcher supports non-static patches (e.g. thcrap); in fact, we would recommend not to use static patches.

You can do so by going to your installation folder and putting the image file (only png files) under the folder named "images" with the file being named "thXX" (for EoSD it would be "th06" and so on...) for the games and "random" for the random button. You will have to enable the feature in the "Settings" section in the Launcher.
